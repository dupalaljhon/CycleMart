📋 How to Fix 401 Unauthorized Error

  1. Click "Clear Token & Logout" button above
  2. Go to your CycleMart application
  3. Click Logout (if still logged in)
  4. Click Login and enter your credentials
  5. Try uploading a product again

❓ Why This Happens

Your current token was created by the old PHP backend with a different secret key. The new Node.js backend cannot verify it. You need to get a fresh token by logging in again.
